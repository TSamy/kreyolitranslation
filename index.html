What we do

Kreyoli Translations & Consulting provides professional translation, interpretation,localization, and language consulting services in Haitian Creole, French, English, Spanish, and more. We help businesses, organizations, and individuals communicate effectively across borders by ensuring every message is accurate, culturally relevant, and impactful. From certified legal documents to corporate presentations, community projects, and global marketing campaigns, we deliver language solutions that connect people, open markets, and drive success.

ABOUT US

At Kreyoli Translations & Consulting, language is more than words, it’s connection, opportunity, and empowerment. Founded by Lee Estane Gine Christie BRUTUS, certified translator and entrepreneur, our women-led company delivers high-quality translation, interpretation, localization, and language consulting in Haitian Creole, French, English, Spanish, and more. With offices in Port-au-Prince and Jérémie, we help Haitian businesses expand beyond borders, access new markets, and share their stories worldwide, guided by our core values of accuracy, cultural sensitivity, and impact. Our Mission: Break language barriers and open doors for Haitian voices in the international marketplace.

MEET OUR FOUNDER

Lee Estane Gine Christie BRUTUS is a certified translator, entrepreneur, AWE alumna, and YLAI 2026 semi-finalist with dual degrees in Management and Linguistics. With over eight years of experience in the language services industry, she founded Kreyoli Translations & Consulting to help Haitian voices resonate worldwide. Passionate about cultural pride and global opportunities, she leads a women-driven mission to break language barriers and empower businesses to thrive internationally.

OUR PORTFOLIO

Our work with NGOs, law firms, entrepreneurs, and international partners showcases our mission: breaking language barriers and amplifying Haitian voices on the world stage.
